Freedom #5—Energy Food
Energy Food teaches you a method for restoring depleted energy in C-1; it is an encoding of the process rather than actual application. To know whether the encoding is accomplished or whether
to repeat the exercise, use the method shortly after each time you
listen to the exercise. If you feel more energized, you have
achieved proficiency with another valuable tool for use in your
daily life. Then move on to the next exercise.
