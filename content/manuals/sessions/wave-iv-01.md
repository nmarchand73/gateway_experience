Adventure #1— One Year Patterning
One Year Patterning requires your thoughtful prior consideration about exactly how and what you desire to be in one year’s
time. Use the same guidelines given for Threshold #3 as you follow the same process. The differences are the longer time frame
and, more importantly, your increased knowledge of your ability
to use the power of expanded consciousness. Be sure you really
want what you pattern for, pattern strongly, and let it go with
trust and confidence and gratitude.
