Odyssey 2—Expansion in Locale 1
Exercise 2 will enable you to begin a greater exploration of Locale
1 through the expansion of your energy body in Focus 12. This is
accomplished by speeding up the vibrations in your energy body
and expanding it outward, like a large bubble surrounding your
physical body. Once fully expanded, you will move your awareness
toward the far reaches of your energy body; becoming lighter as
you move farther from the confines of your physical body.
