Discovery #3 — Advanced Focus 10
Resonant Energy Balloon (REBAL) is a valuable tool to create
a high energy state within and around you. Your REBAL acts as
a magnet, attracting influences you desire and shielding you from
undesired energy. The more you build your REBAL, the better you
sustain higher energy levels. Your REBAL may spontaneously
change form, develop a delicate membrane, or become radiant like
an intensified aura. Although you may not always perceive your
REBAL, play with sensing it through feelings, and/or storing the
energy as in a charged battery.

You can turn it on and use it while in C-1 or in any other state
much as you would turn on a light. Use one resonant energy
breath as the switch. Inhale vibrant energy and, as you hold it,
think of a bright moving circle with the number 10 inside it. Let
the circle surround you as you exhale. Experiment with “popping”
your REBAL while in a group of people and see if its resonance
attracts people who are in harmony with it. Try extending it
around your car to note if you negotiate traffic or find parking
more easily.
