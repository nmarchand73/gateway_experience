Exploring 1—Advanced Focus 12
Focus 12, the state of expanded awareness, can serve as an ideal
springboard for expanded explorations. In this exercise you will
return to Focus 12 to strengthen and reinforce your familiarity
with this valuable state. You are free to explore. You may use this
opportunity to perceive with your nonphysical senses, to establish
new patterns for your life or to reopen channels of communications with your nonphysical friends. Practice your nonverbal communication skills by giving and receiving information.
